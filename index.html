AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

RELIABILITY

To engage in and be exposed to extensive, in depth research and make sure the research is accurate and reliable. Effectively this should backup our opinions and claims with strong evidence as the base of our reasoning.

OBJECTIVITY

Learn to be open minded when discovering new information and aim to look at information objectively before forming an opinion. This will allow emotion and irrational thinking to be segregated from reasoning.

COMMUNICATION

Develop communication skills in the form of writing articles, so that our message is effectively communicated to our target audience, with the help of visual and audio aid.
